Georgia

Georgia is a fictional underwear brand that was designed with the mission to advance the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production.

The product uses skins from discarded peaches from Okanagan, B.C. as the main fibre in the fabric’s production. The branding and the collateral, inspired by peach imagery, cater to women and/or people with vaginas.

Branding by Katarina Yaremkewich. Social Media by Karen Unnsteinsdottir. Packaging by Tin Raganit.

Inclusive Film Finder (IFF)

The Inclusive Film Finder (IFF) is a fictional magazine that amplifies marginalized voices and rates films based on their cultural accuracy and authenticity.

When my team and I surveyed aspiring filmmakers in Capilano University’s Motion Picture Arts program, we identified a problem: the lack of diversity & the portrayal of stereotypes in films.

Our design solution is a film magazine that addresses this by critiquing films based on a rating system that analyzes a film’s cultural authenticity with regard to plot, cast and crew, and costumes.

Layout by Angelica Blanch, Caleb Tsai, and Kyle Rask. Illustrations by Tin Raganit.

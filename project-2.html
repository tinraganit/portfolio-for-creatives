The AuDHD Wombat

The AuDHD Wombat is a 2D character design concept that is inspired by the neurodivergent children I taught as a former art instructor. It explores many neurodivergent people's first experience of ableism, which starts early in the home.

This character began with an ink blot. I saw an animal wearing headphones, which reminded me of my students who would wear noise-cancelling headphones when they were overstimulated. The character sheet consists of poses and expressions based on my students' mannerisms.

The character also wears a weapon that is shaped like a fidget spinner, a toy that many neurodivergent people love. The final illustration is illustrated in a cartoonish style that would appeal to children of ages 6 to 12.
